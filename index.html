Privacy & Safety

Decentralized Research Storage

Your research is stored on a decentralized network, enhancing security and preventing single points of failure. Every paper gains a unique digital signature on the blockchain.

Tamper-Proof Verification

Every paper uploaded receives a unique blockchain timestamp, ensuring the integrity and authenticity of your work and preventing unauthorized edits or plagiarism. This gives you proof of ownership.

End-to-End Encryption

All communications, research papers, and private data are secured with industry-standard end-to-end encryption for maximum confidentiality, ensuring only authorized users can access them.

Access Control & Permissions

You have full control over your research. Choose who can view, comment, or collaborate on your work: Public, Private, or Institution-Only.

Digital NDAs

Researchers can sign Non-Disclosure Agreements digitally within the platform before sharing sensitive work with collaborators, ensuring legal protection.

AI-Powered Plagiarism & IP Protection

Our intelligent system actively detects plagiarism and alerts you if your intellectual property is being misused or copied without permission.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of security to your account. Every login requires a second verification step, preventing unauthorized access.

Anonymous Reporting System

A confidential complaint system allows students to report harassment safely. Complaints are encrypted and accessible only to external legal bodies, human rights organizations, or ethics panels.

Tamper-Proof Evidence Storage & AI Behavior Analysis

Emails, messages, and meeting requests within ResearchBook are automatically logged & time-stamped. AI detects patterns of harassing behavior and securely stores evidence that cannot be deleted or altered, even if the university tries to cover it up.

Direct Access to Legal & Human Rights Support

If a university tries to cover up a case, students can directly connect to Human Rights Commissions, Legal Organizations, and Women's Protection Groups. Universities blocking reports will be publicly flagged.

Protection Against Retaliation

We protect your academic future. ResearchBook stores and protects your research, ensuring that even if you leave an institution due to conflict, your work remains safe, credited to you, and allows you to continue elsewhere.

PhD Harassment & Whistleblower Helpdesk

A confidential portal to report harassment or misuse within the academic community. We prioritize your well-being.

Confidential Submission Option

Submit sensitive research with an option for restricted visibility, allowing you to control who can view your work.

Report Harassment / Misuse

If you encounter any inappropriate content or behavior, please report it immediately.
